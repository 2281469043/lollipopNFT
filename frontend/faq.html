  • What is NFT?

    NFT, or non-fungible token, is a digital asset based on blockchain technology. Unlike homogenous tokens such as Bitcoin, each NFT is unique and represents the ownership of a digital or physical asset. They are often used in fields such as digital art, collectibles, and gaming items, giving the holder the right to verify and transfer the asset.

  • How does the blockchain technology work?

    Blockchain is a decentralized distributed ledger technology that forms a data chain by dividing data into blocks and linking them in chronological order, making the data tamper-proof and transparent. Each transaction will be verified and recorded on multiple nodes to ensure the authenticity and security of the data and avoid single point failures.

  • What are the benefits of using blockchain technology?

    The main advantages of blockchain technology include data transparency, decentralization and security. It allows secure transactions and data sharing without intermediaries, improves transaction efficiency and reduces costs. In addition, the immutability of data guarantees the authenticity and anti-counterfeiting of assets.

  • Are there any risks associated with buying NFT?

    There are certain risks in purchasing NFTs, such as high market volatility, unstable prices, and potential fraud. Since the NFT market is not yet fully regulated, assets may depreciate or be stolen. In addition, the right to hold NFTs is limited to the digital asset itself, not the copyright or usage rights, so you need to carefully understand the terms and conditions before purchasing.
